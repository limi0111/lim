The Villagers in common chase Their Cattle, which it closer raze; And what below the Scythe increased Is pinched yet nearer by the Beast. Such, in the painted World, appeared D’Avenant with th’Universal Herd. They seem within the polished Grass A landscape drawn in Looking-Glass. And shrunk in the huge Pasture show As spots, so shaped, on Faces do. Such Fleas, ere they approach the Eye, In Multiplying Glasses lie. They feed so wide, so slowly move, As Constellations do above. (Marvel, 1651, verse lvii – lviii)
